## 1. OVERVIEW AND METHODOLOGY

This document maps each major contribution of the D-ND framework against existing literature, classifying them as:

- **ORIGINAL**: No direct precedent; conceptually novel contribution to the field
- **ESTENSIONE** (Extension): Generalizes, modifies, or extends a known result to a new domain or formalism
- **RISCOPERTA** (Rediscovery): Result already known, but formulated within D-ND's framework for internal consistency
- **ANALOGIA**: Structural parallel exists in another field; D-ND applies similar principles to a new domain

For each entry, we provide: (1) the D-ND contribution, (2) the most relevant prior work, (3) the classification, and (4) notes for differentiating the D-ND approach in publication.
